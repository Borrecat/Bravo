STRINGER (Code Bravo)

A game based on stringers.
Inspired by "A Shot in the Dark".

HOW TO PLAY:
You will be presented with multiple choices.

EXAMPLE:
1. Start
2. Help

In order to choose an option, type the number and hit enter.

The rewards in game are randomized and the time limit is 1 year.
Every night you will be presented with 3 situations.
Depending on which you choose depends on reward.
Some nights will be dry but some nights will be rich.
The game lasts for 365 nights (1 year) and at the end you unlock a new mode.

Good luck.
